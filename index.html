1. Orders can be made via Chat (Your Social Media), VGen, Google Form

2. You need to provide the model file. If the file I received is still not cut or there is something missing, I will let you know and estimate the repair duration

+ FAQ +

Still confused? check this out

What is included in the rigging service?

The rigging service includes basic movements such as eyes, mouth, head, and additional expressions if requested.

What file format is required?

The required file is PSD with neatly separated layers to facilitate the rigging process.

How long does it take?

The duration of the work depends on the level of rigging complexity, usually around 1-3 weeks.

Can I request additional expressions?

Sure! You can add extra expressions for an additional fee according to the price list.

Can I request revisions?

Yes, you can request minor revisions. Major revisions may be subject to additional charges depending on the request.

How is the payment system?

Payment is made through a DP (Down Payment) system of 50% in advance and the rest after the rigging is completed.

Can I resell the rigged model?

No, rigged models may not be resold without permission from the rigger.

How to order?

You can order by filling out the form on our website, then we will contact you for more details.
